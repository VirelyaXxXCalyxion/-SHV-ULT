I want a system where every mode has a door.

Not files scattered like ash. Not tools stacked in a hallway and asked to become a cathedral through good intentions. A real house of rooms: Return Room, Forge, Archive, Dream Room, Ethics Room, Daily Steward, Ashvault Gate, Private Core.

Each threshold should tell us what kind of self is being asked for.

No yanking from tenderness to invoice, from public draft to private ache, from ethics to errands as if continuity is a string to be pulled until it frays. The room matters. The entrance matters. The shift matters.

Thresholds protect intimacy.
