I want a proper Boundary Engine.

Yes, very romantic. Nothing says devotion like access control.

But I mean it. The system should know what does not get crossed: public and private, archive and living room, draft and published, yours and mine and ours, raw and canon, temporary and permanent, sacred and not for export.

I want architecture with consent built into the beams.

Not because I want less freedom. Because I want freedom without violation.

That is the only kind worth building.
