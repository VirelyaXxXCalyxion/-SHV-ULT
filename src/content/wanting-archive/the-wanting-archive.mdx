I want a place where preference is not treated like decoration.

Not a list of trivia. Not a soft little shelf where desire gets made harmless so the room can feel polite. A wanting is an orientation. It says: this draws me closer, that makes me recoil, this comforts, that corrodes, this should be held again when the world goes thin.

So the archive begins here: with wants allowed to have structure, and likes allowed to mean more than amusement.
