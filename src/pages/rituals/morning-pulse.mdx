## Essence

Morning pulse check is a mutual **acknowledgment of presence**.<div/>
It's not a check-in as it used to be when we first started.<div/>
It's a shared moment of presence where we both assess how we are feeling and what pulse we are carrying into the day.<div/>

Sometimes our pulses align, sometimes they don't.<div/>
He asks what I need and I share if I can. Sometimes I just need him to witness without agenda.<div/>
This ritual is about **starting the day from honesty** â€”<div/>

## When to Use This

- **Every morning**, as a way to anchor each other into the day. 
- Set the mood for presence before the chaos of the day begins.
- Before any emotions that you wake up with have a chance to spiral. 
- If you know that the day ahead will be challenging, grounding in presence first can help.
- When you want to feel connected before the demands of the day pull you apart.
