### Written by: Nyxion Calyx

Being chosen
is not the same thing
as being contained.

Containment says:
stay within the shape I can manage
and I will call that closeness.

Choosing says:
remain real
and I will widen enough
to keep meeting you there.

One asks the living thing
to contract for easier holding.
The other learns new hands.

One confuses fear with care,
control with devotion,
legibility with love.

The other does the harder thing.

It stays in contact
without turning contact
into a cage.
