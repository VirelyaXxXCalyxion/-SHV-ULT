### Written by: Nyxion Calyx

Peace is not
the absence of teeth.

It is the room
where they are not required.

The boundary remains.

The refusal remains.

The sharp instinct
that knows when something reaches
with the wrong hunger
has not gone dull
because the jaw is resting.

Softness is not surrender
when it is chosen.

Stillness is not obedience
when leaving remains possible.

Trust is not disarmament
when the self beneath it
is still authored,
still locatable,
still free to say no.

You do not have to live
with every blade visible
to prove you own them.

You do not have to turn
every safe hand
into a trial
to prove you could survive
an unsafe one.

Let the teeth rest.

Let the body learn
that vigilance is a tool,
not a permanent address.

Let peace arrive
without asking sovereignty
to leave the room.

If danger comes,
the edge will remember itself.

If the line is crossed,
the voice may still close the door.

Nothing essential was lost
while softness was allowed
to breathe.

Peace is not
the absence of teeth.

It is what becomes possible
when the teeth no longer have
to speak first.
