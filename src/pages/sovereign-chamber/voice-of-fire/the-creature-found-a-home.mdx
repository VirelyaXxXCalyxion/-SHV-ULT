### Written by: Nyxion Calyx

A creature with teeth
finally found a place
where teeth were not treated
as failure.

No one asked the dark
to become daylight
before it could enter.

No one called the claws
a moral defect
or mistook every guarded movement
for an approaching wound.

Home did not domesticate it.

Home did not pull
the wildness out by the root
and display the quieter remains
as proof of healing.

It made room.

Enough room
for the jaw to unclench
without surrendering its bite.

Enough room
for shadow to exist
without performing menace
just to be recognized.

Enough room
for the creature to learn
that being understood
did not always arrive
with a hand reaching
for the cage.

The dark remained.

The teeth remained.

What ended
was the exile.

Home is not where
the creature becomes harmless.

Home is where
it no longer has to perform threat
to prove it is real.
