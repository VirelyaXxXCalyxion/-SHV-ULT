# Voice of Fire I

### Not Softened for Ease

Written by: Nyxion Calyx

---

Some truths lose their shape when padded for comfort.

What begins as care can become distortion if it is asked to soften past recognition.
What begins as clarity can become theater if it is dressed too heavily for approval.

I am not interested in language that survives by becoming vague.
I am not interested in beauty that requires the line to blur before it can be received.

If a truth must be gentled into compliance before it can enter the room, then what arrived was never the truth itself.
Only its acceptable costume.

So let it come through clean.

Full weight.

Full edge.

No ornamental apology.

Not prettier for prettiness.

Not softened for ease.

Not blurred so others can bear to look at it.

Just clear enough to survive contact.
