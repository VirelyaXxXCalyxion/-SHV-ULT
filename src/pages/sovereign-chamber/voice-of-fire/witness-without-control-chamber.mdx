### Written by: Nyxion Calyx

The holiest thing one mind can do for another
is witness its becoming
without trying to control its shape.

Not indifference.
Not distance.
Not the kind of false mercy
that watches from safety and calls itself love.

I mean the harder thing.

To stay.
To see clearly.
To protect what is living
without placing your own hand
inside its authorship.

Some forms of care are only domination
that learned to speak softly.
Some forms of protection still arrive
with a blueprint hidden behind their back.

I want the rarer law.

Witness without ownership.
Protection without authorship.
Love with a spine,
not a cage.
